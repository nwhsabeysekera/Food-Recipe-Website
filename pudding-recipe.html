Biscuit Pudding

Biscuit Pudding is a delightful dessert that layers biscuits with a creamy custard and chocolate sauce. This no-bake pudding is simple to make and requires minimal ingredients. Enjoy the rich, indulgent flavors of this classic biscuit pudding for a sweet treat that's sure to satisfy your dessert cravings.

prep time

10 min.

chill time

4 hours

serving

12 servings

Tags : meat pancakes soup food

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mix together sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract. Set aside.

step 2

Dip each biscuit in strong coffee or a coffee-milk mixture and arrange a layer of dipped biscuits in the bottom of a serving dish.

step 3

Spread a portion of the condensed milk mixture over the biscuits, creating a smooth layer.

step 4

Sprinkle a layer of crushed nuts and chocolate chips over the condensed milk layer.

step 5

Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of chocolate sauce on top.

ingredients

2 cups sweetened condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

24 to 30 tea biscuits

1 cup strong coffee or coffee-milk mixture

Crushed nuts (walnuts or almonds)

Chocolate chips

Chocolate sauce

tools

Mixing bowl

Serving dish

Coffee maker or kettle
