Banana Pancakes

Banana Pancakes are a delicious and fluffy breakfast treat that combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with the comfort of pancakes. These easy-to-make pancakes are perfect for a leisurely breakfast or brunch, and they're sure to be a hit with both kids and adults.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags : meat pancakes soup food

instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl, mash ripe bananas using a fork or potato masher until smooth.

step 2

Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract to the mashed bananas. Mix well until all ingredients are combined.

step 3

In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, stirring until just combined.

step 4

Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Spoon the batter onto the griddle to form pancakes. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.

ingredients

2 ripe bananas

2 eggs

1/2 cup milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

tools

Mixing bowl

Fork or potato masher

Griddle or skillet
