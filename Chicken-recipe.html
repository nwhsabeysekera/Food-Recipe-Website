Grilled Chicken

Grilled Chicken is a mouthwatering dish known for its smoky flavor and juicy texture. In this recipe, chicken is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and herbs, then grilled to perfection. Whether you're hosting a barbecue or a casual dinner, this grilled chicken recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : meat pancakes soup food

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.

step 2

Coat the chicken pieces with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to let the flavors infuse.

step 3

Preheat the grill. Grill the marinated chicken for about 10 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.

step 4

Serve the grilled chicken hot with your favorite side dishes.

ingredients

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

1/4 cup olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon paprika

1 teaspoon cumin

Salt and pepper to taste

tools

Grill

Tongs

Bowl for marination
