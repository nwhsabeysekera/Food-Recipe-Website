Spicy Mango Pickle

Spicy Mango Pickle is a traditional condiment made with raw mangoes, spices, and oil. This pickle is known for its bold and tangy flavors, making it a perfect accompaniment to rice dishes, parathas, and more. Enjoy the taste of homemade pickle with this easy-to-follow recipe.

prep time

30 min.

fermentation time

3 days

yield

1 jar

Tags : meat pancakes soup food

instructions

step 1

Wash and dry the raw mangoes. Cut them into small bite-sized pieces, discarding the seed.

step 2

In a large mixing bowl, combine the mango pieces, red chili powder, turmeric powder, fenugreek seeds, and salt. Mix well.

step 3

Heat mustard oil in a pan until it reaches its smoking point. Allow it to cool slightly before adding it to the mango mixture. Mix thoroughly.

step 4

Transfer the pickle to a clean, dry glass jar. Ensure that the pickle is completely submerged in oil to prevent spoilage.

step 5

Close the jar with a tight-fitting lid and place it in a sunny spot for fermentation. Allow it to ferment for at least 3 days, shaking the jar daily to evenly distribute the flavors.

ingredients

3 medium-sized raw mangoes

2 tablespoons red chili powder

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1 tablespoon fenugreek seeds

2 tablespoons salt

1 cup mustard oil

tools

Large mixing bowl

Pan

Glass jar with a tight-fitting lid
