Vegetable Soup

Vegetable Soup is a comforting and nutritious dish filled with a variety of fresh vegetables and savory broth. This easy-to-make soup is perfect for warming up on a chilly day or as a light and healthy meal. Customize it with your favorite vegetables and enjoy a bowl of wholesome goodness.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : meat pancakes soup food

instructions

step 1

In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, celery, and carrots. Cook until vegetables are softened.

step 2

Stir in garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

step 3

Add diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 20 minutes.

step 4

Add your favorite vegetables like peas, corn, and green beans. Cook for an additional 10 minutes or until all the vegetables are tender.

ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 onion, chopped

2 celery stalks, chopped

2 carrots, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 can diced tomatoes

4 cups vegetable broth

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

2 bay leaves

1 cup peas

1 cup corn kernels

1 cup green beans, chopped

Salt and pepper to taste

tools

Large pot

Wooden spoon

Knife and cutting board
