Mushroom Curry

Mushroom Curry is a tantalizing vegetarian dish celebrated for its rich flavors and simplicity. In this Sinhala-inspired recipe, tender mushrooms are cooked in a fragrant blend of spices, infused with coconut milk for a creamy finish. This comforting curry is a delightful addition to your table, offering a burst of flavors that pairs perfectly with rice or bread.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : chicken breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and cinnamon. Let them crackle briefly.

step 2

Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until onions become see-through. Add turmeric and chili powder. Mix well.

step 3

Put in sliced mushrooms. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes.

step 3

Pour in coconut milk. Add salt to taste. Simmer on low-medium heat for 10-15 minutes until mushrooms are soft and the curry thickens a bit.

ingredients

250g mushrooms, sliced

1 onion, finely chopped

2-3 green chilies, sliced

1-inch piece of cinnamon

1 teaspoon- mustard seeds,fenugreek seeds,turmeric powder,chili powder (adjust to taste)

1 cup coconut milk

2 tablespoons oil (coconut oil preferred)

Salt to taste

tools

Pan With Lid and spoon

Measuring Spoons

Cutting Board and Knife
